Forge Student Guide

Everything you need to get started in your classroom.

✔ Presence

The Presence module lets you sign in to class so your teacher knows you're here.

  1. Go to the Presence tab in your classroom.
  2. Press the Sign In button when you arrive.
  3. You'll see a green dot next to your name confirming you're signed in.
  4. The "Who's Here" board shows everyone who is currently present.
  5. When you leave, press Sign Out.
Tip: You can only sign in while a class session is active. If you don't see the Sign In button, ask your teacher to start a session.

✎ Profile

Your profile lets classmates and your teacher learn a little about you.

  1. Go to the Profile tab (or click Edit Profile on the dashboard).
  2. Display Name — the name shown on the smartboard and presence board.
  3. Pronouns — optionally share your pronouns (e.g., they/them).
  4. Theme Color — pick a color for your avatar. Choose a preset or use the color picker.
  5. Ask Me About — list up to 5 topics you're happy to help others with, separated by commas.
  6. Currently Working On — share what you're building or studying. This shows on the smartboard.
Tip: Your profile info appears on the Who's Here board when you're signed in, so keep it up to date!
